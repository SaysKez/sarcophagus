What's That Scratching at Your Sarcophagus?

A micro game of interactive fiction by Kezie Todd

Play as a ghostly pharaoh awakened from your royal slumber by a tomb robber. Unable to interact directly with this vile desecrator of your sanctuary, you must experiment with weird and wacky items and traps to instil fear in the heart of the intruder.

Play Game Download

What's That Scratching at Your Sarcophagus? is an experimental interactive fiction created with Inform. Play right here in your browser or download and use an interpreter such as Zoom for Mac OS X or Windows Frotz.

Created by Kez in a month as part of 12in12: The Side Project Project.
